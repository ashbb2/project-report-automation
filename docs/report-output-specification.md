# Report Output Specification

This file is the single editable source for report output requirements.

## 1) Report format and length
- Paper size: A4
- Font: Arial, 12 pt
- Line spacing: Single
- Paragraph spacing: 6 pt before and 6 pt after
- Main report Chapters 1-8 must total minimum 90 pages
- Chapter 6 must include 15 pages of financial tables inside the 90 pages
- Appendices are generated, but excluded from the 90-page count
- Section 5 must include an additional 5-6 pages of illustrated key equipment profiles inside Chapter 5 (not in appendices)

## 2) Page allocation (final distribution to implement)
Appendices are excluded from the 90 pages.

| Section | Page Target |
|---|---:|
| 1 Executive Summary | 2 |
| 2 Introduction (2.1-2.3 included) | 6 |
| 3 Regulatory Framework (3.1-3.3 included) | 10 |
| 4 Market Assessment (4.1-4.7 included) | 16 |
| 5 Business and Operating Model (includes 5-6 pages equipment images/specs/brands) | 23 |
| 6 Financial Feasibility (includes 15 pages tables within this) | 24 |
| 7 Risk Assessment & Mitigation | 6 |
| 8 Caveats | 3 |
| Total (Ch.1-8 only) | 90 |
| Appendices | Outside 90 |

## 3) Mandatory inclusions
- Chapter 5 includes illustrated key equipment profiles with image links, technical specs, performance/output specs, reputed brands, and source links.
- Chapter 6 includes a dedicated financial table pack aligned to the 15-page requirement.
- Chapter-level content should follow the page intent and structure constraints above.
